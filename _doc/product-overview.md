# MoveCommand — Product Overview

## What It Is

MoveCommand is an all-in-one operations platform designed for moving company managers. It centralises job bookings, crew assignments, and truck logistics into a single real-time command centre — so managers can coordinate every move with confidence and deliver on the promise: on schedule, zero damage.

## Users & Personas

**Primary: Operations Manager / Moving Company Manager**
The person responsible for scheduling jobs, assigning crews and trucks, and ensuring every move is completed on time and without incident. They currently manage this across spreadsheets, phone calls, and disconnected tools — constantly firefighting instead of leading.

## Brand

- **Product Name:** MoveCommand
- **Tagline:** Every move, on schedule. Zero damage.
- **Tone:** Professional, dependable, calm authority. Precise and practical — a tool that works.
- **Palette:** Deep navy (primary), orange (accent/energy), white (base)

## Positioning

The purpose-built operations platform for moving company managers. Where other tools are generic scheduling or project apps, MoveCommand is built around the moving industry's specific workflow: a job comes in, it gets scheduled, a crew and truck are assigned, the move happens, and it is logged complete — with condition records to protect against damage claims.

## Core Features

1. **Job Booking Management** — Create, schedule, and track move jobs from initial customer request through final sign-off. Full job detail: customer info, origin/destination, service date and time, special instructions.
2. **Crew Assignments** — Assign crew members to jobs based on availability. See who is on which job, prevent double-booking, and ensure every move is fully staffed.
3. **Truck Logistics** — Assign trucks to jobs, track fleet availability, and prevent double-assignment. Know which truck is where at any time.
4. **Schedule Dashboard** — A real-time bird's-eye view of all active and upcoming moves. Spot conflicts, gaps, and opportunities at a glance.
5. **Move Progress Tracking** — Track job status from dispatch through completion. Managers always know which moves are in progress, running late, or done.
6. **Condition Logging** — Pre- and post-move item condition records to protect against damage disputes and support zero-damage operations.

## Strategic Goal

Complete every move on schedule with zero damage. MoveCommand exists to make that outcome the default — not the exception.

## Target Outcomes

The platform is designed to drive these measurable improvements for operations teams:

- **On-time delivery rate:** 98%+ moves delivered on schedule with real-time coordination
- **Damage claim reduction:** Up to 80% fewer disputes through structured pre/post-move condition logging
- **Manager time saved:** ~5 hours per week freed from manual coordination and firefighting
- **Fleet utilisation gain:** Up to 35% more jobs completed without adding trucks or headcount

## Scope

MVP covers: job booking management, crew assignments, truck logistics, schedule dashboard, move tracking, and condition logging. Desktop-first for operations managers, with a potential field-facing mobile view in a later phase.
