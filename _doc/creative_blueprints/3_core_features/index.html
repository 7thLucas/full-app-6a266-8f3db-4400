What MoveCommand Does

Built for the Way You Work

Operations Manager
📋

Job Booking Management

Create, schedule, and track move jobs from first call to final sign-off. Full job detail — customer, addresses, date, and special notes.

👷

Crew Assignments

Match crew members to jobs based on availability. Prevent double-booking and ensure every move is fully and correctly staffed.

🚛

Truck Logistics

Assign and track your entire fleet in real time. Know which truck is on which job — no double-assignments, no idle vehicles.

📅

Schedule Dashboard

Bird's-eye view of all active and upcoming moves in real time. Spot conflicts and gaps before they become problems.

📍

Move Progress Tracking

Track every job from dispatch through completion. Always know which moves are in progress, delayed, or done.

🛡️

Condition Logging

Pre- and post-move item condition records protect against damage disputes and keep every move on the path to zero damage.
